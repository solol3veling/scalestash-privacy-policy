Third-Party Social Media API Data

In Short: We collect and use data through social media APIs to enable posting content to your connected social media accounts.

  • Provide analytics and insights about your posted content
  • Manage your connected social media accounts within our platform
  • Ensure successful delivery of your content to the intended platforms

Data Retention for API Data: We retain access tokens and related API data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You can revoke our access to your social media accounts at any time through your social media platform settings or by disconnecting accounts in our platform.

Compliance with Platform Policies: Our use of social media APIs and data complies with each platform's developer policies and terms of service, including Facebook's Platform Policy and LinkedIn's Developer Program Agreement.

10. CONTROLS FOR DO-NOT-TRACK FEATURES

Most web browsers and some mobile operating systems and mobile applications include a Do-Not-Track ("DNT") feature or setting you can activate to signal your privacy preference not to have data about your online browsing activities monitored and collected. At this stage, no uniform technology standard for recognizing and implementing DNT signals has been finalized. As such, we do not currently respond to DNT browser signals or any other mechanism that automatically communicates your choice not to be tracked online. If a standard for online tracking is adopted that we must follow in the future, we will inform you about that practice in a revised version of this Privacy Notice.
